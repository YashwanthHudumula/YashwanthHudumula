## 🚀 About Me

I am a Machine Learning enthusiast currently pursuing a Master's in Computer Science at **Blekinge Institute of Technology**. My interests include **machine learning, deep learning, and computer vision**, and I enjoy building intelligent systems that solve real-world problems.

During my research internship, I worked on deep learning models for **lung cancer detection, segmentation, and classification using CT scan images**, which resulted in a research publication available on **IEEE Xplore**.

I enjoy exploring new AI technologies, working on challenging problems, and collaborating on innovative projects.
